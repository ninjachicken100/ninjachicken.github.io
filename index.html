About Me

I'm currently a Year 2 undergraduate student at Singapore Management University (SMU). My time in SMU so far has got me acquainted with basic Web Development skills, Python programming, and proficiency in analytics using Microsoft Excel. One of the lifelong aspirations i have is to positively impact the lives of people in my capacity professionally and in my day to day life. After obtaining my diploma in Biotechnology, I've came to realize the ability for technology to impact the lives of people across all parts of the world. Hence, i went from working with plants in a laboratory, to dealing with computer codes and application prototyping.
